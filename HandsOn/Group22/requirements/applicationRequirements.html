Application requirements

This application will provide different information of the parks and green spaces of the city of Madrid, having the opportunity of consulting some interesting and relevant information about its flora.

Use cases (the ones explained in the mockups):

  1. Check the information of a park on the map: click on a park in the map so you can see its full name, exact position, the extension in square meters and have acces to the detailed view.
  2. Detailed view: get more information about the park that you have selected, beeing able to consult the species of trees you have at your disposal together with the number of units of each species in that district.
  3. Search engine:

-Tree species consulting: search for the information about a specific tree species, giving information about in which park or district you can find them and the number of specimens available.

-Search for a park or a green space by its name and access to its detailed view.
